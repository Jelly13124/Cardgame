- Skip Yin/Yang and Taiji card art for now.
- Continue the non-Yin/Yang art refactor first.
- Mechanical enemies are allowed, but they must match Cowboy Bill's flat salvage-cartoon styling: large simple shapes, thick outlines, sparse detail, warm brass/brown/grey materials, and one or two bright glow accents.
