Status Effects 状态效果 (13)

流血 Bleed

Take damage equal to stacks at the start of your turn. Then stacks are halved (rounded down).

每回合开始时受到等同层数的伤害。然后层数减半（向下取整）。

灼烧 Burn

Take damage equal to stacks at the end of your turn. Lose 1 stack at the start of each turn.

每回合结束时受到等同层数的伤害。每回合开始时层数 -1。

虚弱 Weak

Outgoing attack damage reduced 25% per stack. Decays 1 per turn.

每层使造成的攻击伤害降低 25%。每回合衰减 1 层。

易伤 Vulnerable

Incoming attack damage increased 50% per stack. Decays 1 per turn.

每层使受到的攻击伤害提高 50%。每回合衰减 1 层。

双倍伤害 Double damage

Next N attacks deal double damage. Consumed on use.

接下来 N 次攻击造成双倍伤害。使用后消耗。

眩晕 Stun

Enemy skips its next turn for each stack (enemy-only).

每层使敌人跳过其下一回合（仅敌人）。

再生 Regen

Heal stacks HP at the start of your turn. Stacks decay by 1 each turn.

每回合开始时回复等同层数的生命值。每回合衰减 1 层。

荆棘 Thorns

When hit by an attack, the attacker takes stacks damage. Loses 1 stack per turn.

被攻击命中时，攻击者受到等同层数的伤害。每回合减少 1 层。

脆弱 Frail

Block gained is reduced 25%. Decays 1 per turn.

获得的格挡降低 25%。每回合衰减 1 层。

闪避 Dodge

Completely negates incoming attacks, one stack consumed per attack.

完全抵消受到的攻击，每次攻击消耗一层。

镀装 Plating

At the start of your turn gain Block equal to stacks. Persistent.

每回合开始获得等同层数的格挡。持续。

镇痛 Numb

Whenever a card is Exhausted gain Block equal to stacks. Persistent.

每当一张牌被消耗时获得等同层数的格挡。持续。

回收 Salvage

Whenever a card is Exhausted draw cards equal to stacks. Persistent.

每当一张牌被消耗时抽取等同层数的牌。持续。

Card Keywords 卡牌关键词 (3)

消耗 Exhaust

The card is removed from the deck for the rest of the combat after it's played.

打出后,该卡在本场战斗中被移除(不再回到牌库)。

保留 Retain

The card is NOT discarded at end of turn — it stays in your hand.

回合结束时不弃掉,保留在手牌中。

重放 Replay

When you play this card, its effects trigger 1 extra time per Replay stack (Replay 1 = it fires twice). Granted by Double-Fire Clip.

打出这张牌时，其效果按「重放」层数额外触发一次（重放 1 = 触发两次）。来源：双发弹夹。

Attributes 属性 (5)

力量 Strength

Each point adds +1 to attack-card damage.

每点 +1 攻击牌伤害。

体质 Constitution

Each point adds +1 to Block gained.

每点 +1 获得的格挡。

智力 Intelligence

Each point: +5% XP gained from combat (level up faster).

每点：战斗获得的经验 +5%（升级更快）。

幸运 Luck

Each point: +3% crit chance, +1.5% loot rarity.

每点:+3% 暴击、+1.5% 战利品稀有度。

魅力 Charm

Each point: -2% shop prices (down to -40%); gates some event options; +2% flee threshold (weakened regular enemies flee, cap 30%).

每点:-2% 商店价格(最低 -40%);解锁部分事件选项;逃跑线 +2%(濒死普通敌人逃跑,上限 30%)。
